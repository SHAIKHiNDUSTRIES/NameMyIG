About.

NameMyIG.rf.gd is a Random Instagram Username Generator web tool that generates random usernames for your Instagram Account.
Usernames Generated on this website is based on a reference file that contains some random words, nouns, adjectives, names, etc.
Usernames generated are based on Instagram's Username Guidlines, such as length of 30 characters only. Letters, Numbers, Periods (.) and Underscores (_) only.
This tool is specially made for the Indian Users, most of the names you will find here are Common Indian names and Surnames.
